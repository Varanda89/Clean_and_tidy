subject - individual used for testing

Activity - It can be:

  *1 WALKING
  
  *2 WALKING_UPSTAIRS
  
  *3 WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS
  
  *4 SITTING
  
  *5 STANDING
  
  *6 LAYING
